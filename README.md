소설 마왕
음악 '마왕', 슈베르트 작곡, 1797년 1월 31일, 이 소설은 병에 걸린 아들이 마왕을 보게 되고 아버지는 그런 아들을 지키려 도망치지만 결국 아들이 죽게 되는 내용이다
The Novel (Poem): "Erlkönig" (The Elf King) Music: "Erlkönig," composed by Franz Schubert (born January 31, 1797) This story depicts a sick son who begins to see the "Elf King" (the personification of death). While his father desperately tries to flee to save him, his efforts are in vain, and the son ultimately passes away.
